## 📌 About

**SyncWearApp** bridges the gap between iOS and Wear OS, solving the long-standing problem of smartwatch compatibility.  

With SyncWearApp, you can:  
- 🔔 **Notification Sync** – Get iOS notifications on your Wear OS watch  
- 📞 **Call Sync** – Receive and manage calls from your wrist  
- 🖼 **Image Sync** – Transfer photos via a built-in local server  
- 👥 **Contact Sync** – Seamlessly import iPhone contacts  

---

## 🎯 Why SyncWearApp?

Wear OS watches don’t officially pair with iPhones. SyncWearApp provides a **first-of-its-kind solution** to make your smartwatch useful, even with iOS.
